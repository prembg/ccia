Why Join The CCIA?

Connections: Enhance your professional stature and advance the field of Cybercrime Investigations. Network and work with the best in the field.

Educational Opportunities: Stay up on cybercrime investigation trends through the CCIA website. Earn CPE credits by attending meetings, webinars, operations, etc.

Public Policy / Advocacy: You can contribute to the strong advocacy program centered on assuring that public safety voices are being heard.​

Free Informational Services: The association keeps members in touch with each other and current issues through the association’s web site and email updates of emerging issues relevant to all forms of cyber investigations.

Networking: You have many opportunities to network with law enforcement and cyber professionals all over the world, building your contacts, sharing ideas, best practices and solutions to enhance your profession, find opportunities for career enhancement, etc.

Discounts on Educational Offerings: You receive reduced registration fees at many CCIA sponsored continuing education events.

Leadership Opportunities: You are provided opportunities to learn and practice leadership and meeting management skills that you may need for your resume, professional growth or self-promotion.

Recognition: You can be recognized or can recognize your colleagues for their achievements in cybercrime investigations, security, or law enforcement.

Empowerment: You are part of a larger community. You are empowered to get involved in issues that affect your personal and professional communities and your quality of life.
