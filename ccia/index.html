News and Information

“Cybercrime Investigators” are not only Federal, State, and Local Law Enforcement Authorities; but Corporate, Small Business that their primary purpose is to protect an infrastructure, or hunt down individuals who want to do harm to their network infrastructures, and Individuals looking to enter the field of Information Technology, Computer Science, Cyber Security, or Forensics. This could come in the form of Cyber Security, Digital or Computer Forensics, Threat Hunting, Etc.​

About CCIA

The Cyber Crime Investigators Association, Inc. or CCIA was formed by a conglomerate of industry professionals in the realm of cyber and digital forensics, intelligence collecting, and cyber security. Based in Gainesville, VA, the core mission of the organization is to provide the most advanced and up to date training, support, and knowledge of cybercrime investigations methodology, techniques, and resources to investigators, analysts, students, and support personnel throughout the world. This includes training and certification in cybercrime investigations, resources for investigations, and support to law enforcement, information technology, and private agencies around the globe.
